Talk Box

June 2019

I was part of a team that created an assistive device (Talk Box) for children with communication problems. The talk box is used by children in Western parts of Kenya. We developed do-it-yourself (DIY) assistive technology prototypes that revolutionized how people with disabilities can access tools that will help them interact with the world. TalkBox allows users to communicate by touching images on an attached surface to play audio files stored within the system. The images and sounds can be customized during assembly, depending on an individual's unique needs. The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) has published the results in IEEE Pervasive Computing.
