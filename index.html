Agriculture for the Youth

November 2019

This web-app is a great place to find information about agriculture in your area, and to run and grow you own agriculture business. I developed an application to eliminate the negative attitude the youth has towards agriculture. The application equips the youth with information about financing, equipment, education, and everything they need to know about getting started in the agriculture sector.
